Peer Mentorship Program (PMP) is a body under the Chief Warden's Office that aims to tackle the issues faced by students in the diverse spheres that affect their academic, social, and general wellbeing. Through this program, we aim to build an effective network for mutual help and benefit of the BITSian community


VISION:
To generate sustained improvement in student capabilities through efficient utilisation of social networks.”

Our Objectives

  1. Providing reliable and comprehensive information and a support system for the student community to eradicate false information, myths, etc surrounding the BITSian student community.
  2. Enabling constructive interaction, guidance and mentorship to build a close-knit student community at BITS Pilani.
  3. Improving the mental health of the campus by working with partner bodies to reduce cases of substance abuse and indiscipline (academic or otherwise) in BITS Pilani.
